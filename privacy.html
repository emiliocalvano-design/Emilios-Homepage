Privacy & analytics

This website keeps simple, self-hosted visitor analytics so I can see how many people read it and where they come from. There are no advertising trackers, and no cookies are set.

What is recorded

For each page view, the site records: your IP address, an approximate location (city / region / country), the network or organisation you connect through (e.g. your internet provider or institution), the page visited, the referring link, and your browser's user-agent string.

Why, and where it is stored

The purpose is to understand readership and traffic trends. The data is stored in my own Cloudflare database and is not sold or shared with third parties. The legal basis is legitimate interest in understanding how the site is used (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

Your rights

As IP addresses can be personal data under the GDPR, you may ask me to access or delete the records relating to you. To do so, contact me at the address on the CV & Contact page. Older records are deleted periodically.
